About Butterfly Hearts Charity

Founded by C. Memuci Kirgotty, Butterfly Hearts Charity is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming lives through acts of kindness, empowerment, and sustainable community development. Based in Kenya, we focus on uplifting vulnerable communities with special emphasis on children, women, and youth. We believe that small acts of compassion can create a ripple effect of hope and progress.
